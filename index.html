After years of discipline and dedication I have realized the possibilities with Javascript are endless. With this creative freedom along with my passion for design and a minimalist approach, I aim to give you a clean and modern application. Most recently, I have been striving to learn web 3.0 and build immersive applications onto a blockchain. Stay tuned!
